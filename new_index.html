Jaisidh Singh

Deep learning researcher.

I am a Master's in Machine Learning student at the University of Tübingen. Previously, I haved interned at Bosch Research India with Dr. Amit A. Kale and Sonam Singh. I've also worked with Dr. Richa Singh, Dr. Mayank Vatsa, and Dr. Aparna Bharati during my undergrad at IIT Jodhpur, majoring in AI and Data Science.


As a researcher, I'm interested in a broad range of problems pertaining to how and what neural networks learn. To this end, I am currently working with ELLIS and SAIR Montreal on hyper-networks for efficient training of for neural networks.

In the past, my research has explored what multi-modal models learn through the lens of compositionality, and mitigating harmful effects of training image-generation models from the perspective of interpretability.
